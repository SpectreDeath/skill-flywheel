# SKILL: Web3 Decentralized Finance (DeFi) Development

## Purpose
Comprehensive development of decentralized finance protocols, including lending platforms, DEXs, yield farming, and financial primitives for blockchain ecosystems.

## When to Use

- Building decentralized lending and borrowing protocols
- Creating automated market makers (AMMs) and DEX platforms
- Developing yield farming and liquidity mining systems
- Implementing synthetic assets and derivatives
- Building decentralized insurance protocols
- Creating stablecoin mechanisms and algorithms
- Developing financial primitives and composability layers

## When NOT to Use

- Traditional centralized financial applications
- Regulatory-compliant financial services requiring KYC/AML
- High-frequency trading systems requiring sub-millisecond latency
- Projects in jurisdictions with strict DeFi regulations
- When blockchain technology doesn't provide clear financial advantages

## Edge Cases and Troubleshooting

### Edge Case 1: Oracle Manipulation
**Problem**: Price feed manipulation affecting protocol security
**Solution**: Multi-oracle redundancy, time-weighted averages, and manipulation detection

### Edge Case 2: Impermanent Loss
**Problem**: LP providers experiencing losses due to price volatility
**Solution**: Dynamic fee structures, insurance mechanisms, and hedging strategies

### Edge Case 3: Regulatory Compliance
**Problem**: Navigating complex and evolving DeFi regulations
**Solution**: Jurisdiction-specific access controls and compliance monitoring

### Edge Case 4: Smart Contract Upgrades
**Problem**: Need to update protocols without disrupting user funds
**Solution**: Proxy patterns with proper migration and testing procedures
