## Purpose

Achieve correct answers efficiently, particularly under time pressure such as standardized tests.

## Description

Accuracy and speed involves balancing speed with correctness through strategic approaches: eliminating wrong answers, using shortcuts, time-boxing questions, and efficient calculation techniques.

## Examples

### Test Strategy

```
EASY: Answer in <30 seconds (20% of questions)
MEDIUM: Answer in 60-90 seconds (60% of questions)
HARD: Flag and return, max 2 minutes (20% of questions)
```
