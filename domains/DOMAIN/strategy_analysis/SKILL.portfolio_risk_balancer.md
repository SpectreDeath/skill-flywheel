# SKILL: Portfolio Risk Balancer

## Purpose

Apply Ralph Wiggum-style maximum chaos principles to optimize portfolio risk management by injecting unpredictable yet strategically coherent disruptions. This skill breaks conventional risk analysis patterns by generating chaotic interventions that reveal hidden risk correlations, unexpected diversification opportunities, and novel portfolio balancing strategies that traditional analysis cannot detect.

## When to Use

- Conventional portfolio risk analysis reveals no clear optimization opportunities
- Need to identify hidden risk correlations or unexpected diversification benefits
- Exploring boundary conditions and extreme scenarios in portfolio management
- When "thinking outside the box" is explicitly required for risk strategy
- Breaking through analytical plateaus in portfolio optimization

## When NOT to Use

- When stable, predictable portfolio management is required for immediate decisions
- In safety-critical financial situations where chaos could cause significant harm
- When following established risk management protocols is essential
- During emergency portfolio responses requiring immediate, predictable strategies
- When portfolio stability is more important than discovering new risk insights

## Inputs

- **Required**: Current portfolio configuration and risk profile
- **Required**: Chaos parameters and entropy injection levels for risk disruption
- **Required**: Constraints and boundaries for chaotic portfolio exploration
- **Optional**: Historical chaos outcomes and pattern analysis from previous optimizations
- **Optional**: Portfolio resilience and adaptability metrics
- **Assumptions**: Chaos can reveal hidden risk insights, portfolio can handle unpredictable analysis, recovery from chaotic states is possible

## Outputs

- **Primary**: Chaotic portfolio risk insights and novel balancing approaches
- **Secondary**: Entropy analysis and chaos impact assessment on portfolio stability
- **Tertiary**: Recovery strategies and portfolio stabilization procedures
- **Format**: JSON structure with chaotic outputs, entropy metrics, and standardization procedures

3. **Creative Risk Disruption**: Inject novel approaches that break conventional risk analysis patterns
4. **Edge Case Portfolio Exploration**: Analyze boundary conditions and extreme scenarios in portfolio systems
5. **Recovery Strategy Development**: Plan for portfolio system stabilization after chaotic interventions
