# SKILL: Scenario Planning Simulator

## Purpose

Apply Ralph Wiggum-style maximum chaos principles to optimize scenario planning by injecting unpredictable yet strategically coherent disruptions. This skill breaks conventional scenario analysis patterns by generating chaotic interventions that reveal hidden future states, unexpected outcome probabilities, and novel planning frameworks that traditional analysis cannot detect.

## When to Use

- Conventional scenario planning reveals no clear future opportunities or threats
- Need to identify hidden outcome probabilities or unexpected future states
- Exploring boundary conditions and extreme scenarios in strategic planning
- When "thinking outside the box" is explicitly required for future strategy
- Breaking through analytical plateaus in scenario optimization

## When NOT to Use

- When stable, predictable scenario planning is required for immediate decisions
- In safety-critical strategic situations where chaos could cause harm
- When following established scenario planning protocols is essential
- During emergency planning requiring immediate, predictable outcomes
- When strategic stability is more important than discovering new future insights

## Inputs

- **Required**: Current scenario planning configuration and future state analysis
- **Required**: Chaos parameters and entropy injection levels for scenario disruption
- **Required**: Constraints and boundaries for chaotic scenario exploration
- **Optional**: Historical chaos outcomes and pattern analysis from previous simulations
- **Optional**: Strategic system resilience and adaptability metrics
- **Assumptions**: Chaos can reveal hidden future insights, system can handle unpredictable analysis, recovery from chaotic states is possible

## Outputs

- **Primary**: Chaotic scenario planning insights and novel future frameworks
- **Secondary**: Entropy analysis and chaos impact assessment on strategic planning
- **Tertiary**: Recovery strategies and scenario stabilization procedures
- **Format**: JSON structure with chaotic outputs, entropy metrics, and standardization procedures

## Capabilities

1. **Maximum Entropy Scenario Generation**: Create completely unpredictable scenario planning analyses
2. **Chaos Pattern Analysis**: Identify beneficial patterns within chaotic strategic system behavior
3. **Creative Scenario Disruption**: Inject novel approaches that break conventional analysis patterns
4. **Edge Case Scenario Exploration**: Analyze boundary conditions and extreme scenarios in planning systems
5. **Recovery Strategy Development**: Plan for strategic system stabilization after chaotic interventions
