## When to Use

- Breaking out of optimization plateaus and local maxima across all strategy domains
- Need to identify hidden strategic opportunities or unexpected system vulnerabilities
- Exploring boundary conditions and extreme scenarios in strategic systems
- When "thinking outside the box" is explicitly required for strategic breakthroughs
